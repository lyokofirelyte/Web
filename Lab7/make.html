Estimated Semester Expenses

Starting in the Fall 2014, tuition at Thomas Jefferson College will be $200 a credit hour. To calculate your estimated expenses for the semester, select the number of credit hours from drop down list. Estimage your monthly expenses such as room and board (rent), entertainment, and other personal expenses. For example, if dorm room and board is $850 a month, your dorm expense is $3,825 a semester. The average costs of books is between $600 to $950 a semester. You might expect to have about $1300 to $1500 personal expenses a semester. These estimates do not include things such as travel or any emergencies that may occur. Total all expenses and enter in the Other Total Expenses box.
